The Bad

The desktop software is completely made in house, which is a bold step in our view but it has its share of bugs and glitches, which are a big turn off for many players who flock to play online poker on PokerBaazi religiously.

The desktop poker client gets updated daily, sometimes more than once a day and takes a few minutes to load which is quite frustrating to see on a daily basis.

Pokerbaazi doesn’t have a dedicated app for iPad which can be troublesome for some players.

We found that OFC tables run only on the mobile client and not on the desktop one, which frankly makes PokerBaazi lag way behind their competitors.

Pokerbaazi also adds a separate GST on top of the rake charged in cash games. In an already tough secluded Indian poker field, where the government takes a massive chunk in 30% TDS charges, players finding it difficult to just break even, these petty charges do nothing to benefit the average online poker enthusiast.
